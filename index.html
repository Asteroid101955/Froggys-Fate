Welcome to Froggy's Fate!

Froggy's Needs:

  • Fullness: Feed your froggy flies from the jar in the kitchen. Drag the fly jar to the frog.
  • Cleanliness: Clean up poop when it appears. Click on the poop to remove it. You can also wash your froggy in the bathroom.
  • Happiness: Play with your froggy using the ball in the playroom. Drag the ball to the frog.
  • Sleep: Let your froggy rest in the bedroom. Click the bed to make it sleep.
  • Oxygen: Open the window to let in fresh air. Click the window to open/close it.
  • Temperature: Adjust the furnace to keep your froggy warm. Click the furnace to increase the temperature.

Game Over:

The game ends if any of your froggy's stats drop to zero, or if the temperature gets too cold (below -10°C) or too hot (above 35°C).

Good luck, and have fun!
